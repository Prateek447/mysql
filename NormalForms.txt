
1->Necessary steps to normalize 1NF:
 i>Each cell should have Single value.
 ii>Enteries in a column are same type.
 iii>Rows should identified uniquely.

2->Necessary steps to normalize 2NF:
 i>Table should be in 1NF.
 ii>All attributes(Non-Key Columns)dependent on the key
 
3->Necessary steps to normalize 3NF:
 i>Table should be in 2NF.
 ii>All Fields(Columns) can be determined Only be the key
   in the table and no other column.
